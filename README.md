## 🌟 What Is Refueler?

**Refueler** is not just another productivity app.

It is a complete focus system built to help users remember **why they need to lock in**.

Instead of only blocking distractions, Refueler combines:

* 🔒 App blocking
* 🎯 AI-powered goal planning
* 🎥 Personal motivation videos
* 🔥 A growing flame companion
* 📊 Focus analytics
* 🏆 Streaks, rewards, and milestones
* ⚡ Motion-based Lock-In challenges

> **From dream → plan → success.**

## 🚀 Mission

Refueler helps people stop giving up on their dreams.

Most apps tell you to focus.
Refueler reminds you **what you are focusing for**.

Whether you are studying, building a business, training, applying to college, or chasing a long-term dream, Refueler helps you turn ambition into structure.

### Refueler helps users:

* 🧠 Protect focus from distracting apps
* 📅 Turn dreams into daily plans
* 🔥 Build momentum through streaks
* 🎥 Stay motivated by people they care about

# 🛠️ Technical Highlights

Refueler is built with a privacy-first approach.

### High-Level Technical Areas

* 📱 Apple Screen Time API integration
* 🔒 App blocking through FamilyControls, ManagedSettings, and DeviceActivity
* 🧠 On-device blocking logic
* ☁️ Cloud sync for goals and progress
* 🔐 Apple Sign-In and Email OTP authentication
* 🗄️ Supabase backend support
* 🔗 Deep linking for sharing and widgets
* 📲 Widget support for focus, tasks, motivation, and progress

Sensitive implementation details are intentionally kept private.

# 🔐 Privacy
